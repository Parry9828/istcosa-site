About Us

The Indo Swiss Old Students’ Association (ISTCOSA) was formed to bring the alumni under one umbrella. Since then the association has risen from being merely a gathering for various activities like lunch and dinner Get Together to a strong fraternity with goals and achievements. Members and friends in different parts of the world with a will to promote fraternity gathered and helped set the goals and formulated methods to achieve set goals. The governing body is elected with members having positive social experiences.

Main goals of the association are:

To promote honorable friendship, intellectual excellence, mutual aid, close personal connection between alumni, old students and colleagues to bind them together for mutual interest.

To inculcate courage, self control, obedience and courtesy towards all with whom they may come in contact with.

Support alumni by providing them with business or employment and also letting them outsource products, services and employment.

Manage and increase the resources of the fraternity which include membership, alumni involvement, assets and revenues, communications and technology orientation.

To impart information about latest manufacturing processes, trade fairs and exhibitions.

To raise a rolling fund for the welfare of alumni and to institute scholarships and loans for deserving students.

MEMORANDUM Name - The name of the Association shall be INDO-SWISS TRAINING CENTRE OLD STUDENTS ASSOCIATION (ISTCOSA) Registered Office – The Registered office of the ASSOCIATION shall be in the Union Territory of Chandigarh and at present shall be at ISTC Hostel, Indo-Swiss Training Centre, CSIO, Sector-30, Chandigarh. Area of Operation – Entire Union Territory of Chandigarh.

Aims & Objectives

To promote and foster mutually beneficial interaction within the alumni and between the Alumni & present students of ISTC.

To encourage the formation of Chapters/ Coordinators as a means to increase the participation of Alumni.

To encourage the Alumni to take an active and abiding interest in the work and progress of the Institute so as to contribute towards enhancement of the social utility of their Alma Mater.

To organize and establish scholarship/ Award/ Reward funds to help the needy and deserving students.

To institute prizes and awards for outstanding & innovative works or other professional activity by the Alumni or present students of the Institute; also to suitably recognize outstanding social and community service by the Alumni and the students.

To undertake to organize activities of a civic or charitable nature so as to increase public awareness of the role of technology in value addition in the economic and social development of the nation.

To invest and deal with the funds & moneys of the Association.

To do all such other lawful things as are conducive or incidental to the attainment of the above objectives and/or beneficial to the interests of the Institute and its Alumni.

To take advantage of developing technologies like the internet in achieving the aims and objectives of the Association.

To provide whatever assistance possible during natural calamities like floods, earthquakes, famine etc.

To organize charitable & social activities like blood donation, free medical check-up camps etc.

OBJECTIVES ANCILLARY TO THE ACHIEVEMENT OF MAIN OBJECTIVES :

To create, takeover, alter and maintain buildings for the purpose of the Association.

To do such other things/ acts/ activities which are necessary and may be incidental conducive to the attainment of the objectives of the Association.

To acquire by purchase, donation or otherwise, any movable and/or immovable properties, rights, interests and titles in the interest of the ASSOCIATION and its objectives and to manage them.

To cooperate and affiliate with other institutions whose objectives are similar to those of the Association.

To form Committees & Sub-Committees for the attainment of aims & objectives of the Association.

ISTCOSA REGISTRATION

ISTCOSA is registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 with following Terms & Conditions

All the income, earning, moveable & immovable properties of the Association shall be solely utilized and applied towards the promotion of its aims and objectives only, as set forth in the Memorandum of Association and no profit on thereof shall be paid or transferred, directly or indirectly by way of dividends, bonus, profits or any manner whatsoever to the present or past members of the association or to any person claiming through any one or more of the present or past members. No member shall claim any movable or immovable properties of the association or make any profits whatsoever, by virtue of this membership.

No member of the Governing Body/ Executive Committee of the Association shall be appointed to any salaried office of the Association and no remuneration shall be given by the Association to any member of such Governing Body/ Executive.

Committee except repayment of out-of-pocket expenses and interest on money lent or spent for the Association.

The association, by its constitution, is required to apply its profits, if any or other income in promoting its objectives.

If upon winding up or dissolution of the Association, there remains, after realization of its debts & liabilities, any property.

whatsoever, the same shall not be paid to or distributed among the members of the Association but shall be given or transferred to some other institution having objectives similar to the objectives of the Association and to be determined by the members of the Association at or before the time of dissolution.
